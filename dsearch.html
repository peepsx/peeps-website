Find Freedom.

dSearch is a search engine that allows users to search the decentralized web inside and out, without sacrificing their privacy or their rights in the process. dSearch launches September 1st, 2020.

keyboard_arrow_down

Photograph of Hawaii's Lulumalu Falls

search the dweb.

dSearch allows users to search dSites and dApps on the dWeb, as well as images, posts on dSocial, news on dNews, videos on dVideo and more - safely and privately.

keyboard_arrow_down

search with actual privacy.

dSearch will never spy on your searches, nor is there a record of what you've searched for, therefore, you can search with actual privacy.

keyboard_arrow_down

a new kind of search engine.

dSearch uses a game-changing technology stack that allows dSearch to perform greater than search engines like Google, without a single data center or even a server. dSearch instead operates across the computers that use it, further increasing the privacy of its users.

keyboard_arrow_down

search and earn.

dSearch users can opt-in to view ads that are relevant to their searches via our dAdvertising platform and can even earn a share of the platform's ad revenue.
